# BIZZY AND THE GREAT UNSPELLING
### Spelling Quest reboot — one saga, 15 chapters, 3 acts. A hero's journey where WORDS ARE POWER.

---

## THE PREMISE (one breath)
Words hold the world together. **Vex**, a silver-tongued hornet cast out of the Hive Academy for twisting words, has built the **Unspelling Engine** — a machine that eats letters out of the world. Rivers forget how to "flow". Doors forget "open". Bees forget their own names. One young bee — **Bizzy** — escapes the first attack, and chapter by chapter gathers a crew of friends, each carrying one of the five Word Arts (Spelling, Meaning, Knowledge, Speed, Sayings). Vex gathers his own gang in parallel. It ends in a two-chapter face-off for every word in the world.

**Why it works for a spelling app:** the villain literally UN-spells the world; every game mechanic is Bizzy RE-spelling it. Spelling isn't a quiz bolted on — it's the superpower.

## THE 15 CHAPTERS

### ACT I — THE GATHERING (Ch 1–5) · "the meadow falls, the crew forms"
**Ch 1 · Escape from the Meadow of Challenges** — The Unspelling hits at dawn: flowers lose their names and turn grey. Bizzy must escape the greying meadow.
🎮 **HONEYCOMB RUN** (Pac-Man engine): 3-minute survival in a honeycomb maze. Grey moths patrol. Collect nectar dots for points; reach the target score to escape. **Golden flowers** appear every ~20s — touching one opens a quick spell-a-word card; correct = big point bonus + the flower blooms back into color (the maze visibly heals as you spell).

**Ch 2 · The Long Sky** — The meadow is lost. Bizzy flies for the Hive with the Smudge on her tail, and rescues Bumble from a grey-out mid-flight.
🎮 **KEEP FLYING** (dino/flappy engine): tap/space to stay airborne through wind, thorns and moth swarms. **Land on 10 honey pots** to complete the run — each pot is a spell-a-word stop (word gets you honey fuel; miss costs altitude, not death). Recruit: Bumble.

**Ch 3 · The Great Bee Race** — The Hive won't believe a kid. The elders set a test: outfly the three fastest bees alive.
🎮 **BEE GRAND PRIX** (race engine): side-view race vs Bumble, Waggle & Drone (3 laps). Spelling = speed: word cards stream by; answering correctly fires a nitro boost. Win the race, win the crew. Recruit: Waggle & Drone.

**Ch 4 · The Owl Who Knew Too Much** — Professor Hoot has mapped the Unspelling — but his library's index has been eaten. Restore it to learn where Vex strikes next.
🎮 **THE INDEX RELAY** (trivia engine, re-skinned): timed trivia relay through the library stacks — every 3 correct answers re-shelves a wing and opens the next aisle (existing trivia bank, quest wrapper + streak stakes). Recruit: Hoot.

**Ch 5 · The Smudge** — First real battle: the moth-cloud descends on the Hive gates.
🎮 **BOSS: SPELL-SHIELD** — the Smudge hurls scrambled words; spell them straight to turn each one into a shield hex. Fill the shield wall before the timer to drive it off. Twist ending: the Smudge whispers — *"He knows your name, little bee."* Recruit: Sting (defects mid-battle).

### ACT II — THE RISING SHADOW (Ch 6–10) · "Vex builds his army; the crew is tested and broken"
**Ch 6 · The Web** — The crew crosses the Old Orchard; WebWeaver's web is the only bridge — but she types her silk, and the Unspelling has stolen her keys.
🎮 **TYPE-THE-SILK** (typing engine): type falling letter-threads before they hit the ground; complete words weave bridge segments. 60-second waves, existing typing trainer re-skinned. Recruit: WebWeaver.

**Ch 7 · The Parrot's Bridge** — Echo guards the canyon crossing and speaks ONLY in idioms. Prove you speak Sayings.
🎮 **IDIOM STONES** (idiom deck, gamified): stepping stones across the canyon — each stone shows an idiom with a missing word; pick/spell it right to step. Wrong = stone wobbles (2 wobbles = restart the crossing). Recruit: Echo.

**Ch 8 · THE NIGHT EVERYTHING WENT QUIET** ⚡ *the dark chapter* — Vex raids the camp. **Bumble and Hoot are captured.** Bizzy's own name starts greying on her wing. Stealth, not force.
🎮 **SILENT MAZE** (Pac-Man engine, night mode): the maze is dark (small light radius), Gnash's locust patrols sweep in cones. No points — pure stealth to the exit. Golden flowers now RESTORE BIZZY'S NAME letter by letter: spell 5 words hidden in the dark to keep her from fading. Fail state ends with rescue by Luma's light — hope beat.

**Ch 9 · The Meaning of Light** — Luma teaches the deepest Word Art: a word you don't understand is a word Vex can steal back.
🎮 **THE POTION OF SENSE** (vocab engine): brew light-potions by matching words to meanings under time; each correct pour brightens the room; brew 12 to forge the **Lantern of Meaning** (weapon for the finale). Recruit: Luma.

**Ch 10 · GNASH** — Rescue mission into the locust camp to free Bumble & Hoot.
🎮 **BOSS: THE CHASE** (race engine, inverted): Gnash pursues; spell fast to stay ahead through 3 escalating stages; final stage lands the crew at the cages — free friends by spelling their NAMES. Ends: Vex speaks to Bizzy for the first time — offers her a place at his side. She refuses. He shows her the Engine, nearly complete. *"Then you'll watch the last word die."*

### ACT III — THE FACE-OFF (Ch 11–15) · "assemble everything; spend everything"
**Ch 11 · The Queen's Answer** — Return to the Hive; the Queen commits the Guard. The full alliance stands.
🎮 **MUSTER OF THE SWARM** (mixed gauntlet, light): 3 short waves — one maze, one flight, one race — each themed as marshalling a division. Every recruited friend's power is active from here on.

**Ch 12 · The Word Vault** — To fight the Engine you need ammunition: the old Vault of First Words, sealed by the hardest spellings in the world.
🎮 **VAULT LOCKS** (spelling, championship mode): 5 locks, each a themed word set (silent letters, doubles, foreign roots — pulls from Ahana's actual weak-pattern radar). Earn the **First Words** (finale power-ups).

**Ch 13 · March on the Engine** — The approach: a scorched valley where the Unspelling is total. Everything is grey; the crew's powers flicker.
🎮 **THE GREY GAUNTLET** (flight engine, hard): the long flight in — obstacles are now un-spelled objects flickering in and out; honey pots are guarded (trivia OR idiom OR typing challenge at each — player's choice, playing to their strongest Art).

**Ch 14 · THE FACE-OFF, PART I — The Static** ⚡ *Infinity-War beat* — Assault on the Engine. The crew splits to breach the walls; Bizzy reaches the core and faces The Static.
🎮 **BOSS: THE STATIC** — three-phase: (1) maze through the Engine's guts, (2) type-storm as it screams scrambled words, (3) spell-duel — but the fight CANNOT be won. Scripted gut-punch: the Engine fires, the screen greys out, friends' portraits fade one by one. Chapter "ends in defeat" — cliffhanger. (Player always "loses" here — it's story, and the game says so afterward: *to be continued…*)

**Ch 15 · THE FACE-OFF, PART II — Every Word Ever** ⚡ *Endgame beat* — Bizzy alone in the grey. One thing survived: the words SHE learned — every word ever spelled in this app is stored in her Quill. She speaks them; friends spell back into existence one by one (each returns with their game: a race beat, a flight beat, a web beat...). Full crew restored → final duel with Vex.
🎮 **FINALE: THE RESPELLING** — five-stage medley, one stage per Word Art: maze (Spelling) → flight (Courage) → race (Speed) → mixed quiz (Knowledge/Sayings) → last stand: face Vex in a pure spelling duel where every correct word visibly re-colors the world, ending on one final word — **"together"**. Vex isn't destroyed; his words are returned to him, and he remembers why he loved them. Massive celebration, saga badge, credits with every character.

## THE THREE NEW GAME ENGINES
| Engine | Chapters | Core loop | Spelling hook | Scaling |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **HONEYCOMB MAZE** (Pac-Man-like) | 1, 8, 11, 14, 15 | swipe/arrow movement, patrols, dots, 3-min survival | golden flowers → word card → points/heal/name-letters | patrol count/speed, light radius, word tier |
| **KEEP FLYING** (flappy/dino) | 2, 11, 13, 15 | tap to fly, obstacles, honey-pot landings | each pot = word stop (10/run) | gap size, wind gusts, guarded pots |
| **BEE GRAND PRIX** (racer) | 3, 10, 11, 15 | 3-lap side-view race, boost management | word cards = nitro; rivals rubber-band | rival AI, hazard density, word tier |
Existing engines re-skinned into the saga: trivia (4, 13), typing (6, 14), idioms (7, 13), vocab (9), championship spelling (12, 15).

## INTEGRATION RULES
- **Words come from the live pool engine** (same corpus-backed difficulty pools as Practice) so Quest reinforces exactly what Ahana is learning; Vault Locks (Ch 12) pull from her personal weak-pattern radar.
- **Friends = modes**: each recruit personifies one learning mode and their power-up only helps if that mode's challenges are played — gentle pressure to touch trivia/typing/idioms/vocab, never a wall.
- **Economy**: chapter clear = coins + karma as today; saga adds **Story Gems** (1/chapter, 15 total) displayed on a saga shelf; finale badge "Every Word Ever."
- **Progression**: chapters strictly sequential (this is now one story); replays of cleared chapters allowed for better stars; existing 14 avatar-pack "seasons" become **Side Tales** (kept, unchanged, clearly labeled) so nothing built is lost.
- **Difficulty**: all arcade games have easy/medium/hard/champ (existing picker); story never blocks on arcade skill — 3 failed attempts offers "Story Assist" (shorter survival, wider gaps) so a 9-year-old always reaches the next beat.
