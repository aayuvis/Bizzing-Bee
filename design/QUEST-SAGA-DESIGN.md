# BIZZY AND THE GREAT UNSPELLING
### The 30-chapter saga — 5 acts, 15 worlds, every chapter a real game.

## THE PREMISE
Words hold the worlds together. **Vex**, a silver-tongued hornet expelled from the Hive Academy for twisting words, builds the **Unspelling Engine** — it eats letters out of reality. Fifteen worlds start greying out, one by one. Bizzy the bee escapes the first attack and crosses all fifteen worlds, recruiting each world's champion — while Vex corrupts a rival in parallel. Chapters 29–30 are the two-part face-off for every word ever.

**Design law #1: the villain UN-spells the world; every game is Bizzy RE-spelling it.** Spelling is the superpower, not the pop quiz.
**Design law #2: every chapter is a GAME a kid would play for fun even without the story.**

# THE 30 CHAPTERS

## ACT I · THE SCATTERING (1–6) — *the meadow falls; the first crew forms*

**1 · Escape from the Meadow of Challenges** — dawn attack; flowers grey out mid-name.
🎮 **HONEYCOMB RUN** *(new engine: Pac-Man)* — top-down honeycomb maze, swipe/arrow controls. Survive 3:00 and bank the target nectar score. Grey moths patrol on paths; nectar dots = 10 pts. **Golden flowers** spawn every ~20s → tap opens a 10s spell-card → correct = 150 pts + the maze visibly re-blooms around you (a ring of grey cells turns to color — the core fantasy in one image). Power pellet = royal jelly (moths flee 6s). Scaling: moth count/speed, dot targets, word tier. Assist after 3 fails: 2:00 survival, slower moths.

**2 · The Long Sky** — flight to the Hive with the Smudge on Bizzy's tail; Bumble rescued mid-air.
🎮 **KEEP FLYING** *(new engine: flappy/dino hybrid)* — tap/space to beat wings; wind gusts push, thorn walls scroll. **Land on 10 honey pots** to finish; each pot = a spell-word stop (correct = honey fuel + pot banked; miss = lose altitude, pot re-queues later — never a dead end). Hidden bonus: a cloud-letter trail that spells a secret word for a gem.

**3 · The Elders' Test: The Bee Grand Prix** — the Hive won't follow a kid; outfly the three fastest bees.
🎮 **BEE GRAND PRIX** *(new engine: side-view racer)* — 3 laps vs Bumble, Waggle, Drone Dan (rubber-band AI). Word cards stream at the top; answer correctly any time to bank **nitro** (up to 3); tap to burn. Pollen clouds slow you; flower arches give small boosts. Win = crew joins.

**4 · The Queen's Riddle** — the Hive Queen tests wisdom, not wings: "One long word holds a hundred short ones."
🎮 **WORD HIVE** *(new engine: anagram builder — "make words from the big word")* — a 9–12 letter word (e.g. THUNDERSTORM) in a honeycomb rack; build **20 smaller words** from its letters before the honey timer drains. Drag letters or type. 3-letter = 1 cell, longer = more. Duplicates rejected, proper nouns rejected; live counter fills a comb. This is the classic bee-favorite word game, saga-framed.

**5 · Whack-the-Moths** — the Smudge's swarm infiltrates the Hive nursery at night.
🎮 **WHACK-A-MOTH** *(new engine: whack-a-mole)* — moths pop from comb cells carrying letters. A target word shows at top: **whack its letters in order** (wrong moth = -time; golden moth = bonus). 8 words per round, speeding up. Pure tap-frenzy fun; spelling order is the skill.

**6 · BOSS: The Smudge** — first true battle at the Hive gates.
🎮 **SPELL-SHIELD** *(boss engine)* — the Smudge hurls scrambled words in waves; unscramble-and-type each to convert it into a shield hex; build the wall before the gate timer. Final phase: it dives — 3 quick dictation words (voice only) to fire the Stinger Quill. Exit line: *"He knows your name, little bee."* **Sting** the wasp defects to the crew.

## ACT II · THE WORLD TOUR (7–12) — *recruiting champions; the traitor falls*

**7 · Stage World: The Show Must Go On** — the Blackout hits mid-performance; Melody's sheet music greys.
🎮 **SPOTLIGHT SIMON** *(new engine: memory sequence)* — spotlights flash letters on stage tiles in sequence (starts at 3, grows to 9); repeat the sequence — and every completed sequence IS a word, which you then spell once more from memory with tiles hidden. Melody & Maestro join.

**8 · Cosmos: The Scrambled Constellations** — Void has shuffled the stars; the sky spells nonsense.
🎮 **UNSCRAMBLE THE STARS** *(new engine: anagram)* — constellations appear as scrambled letter-stars; drag stars into order to re-form the word; correct = the constellation line-art draws itself and glows. 12 words, escalating length. Astro, Comet & Zib join.

**9 · Dojo: The Thousand Cuts** — Panda Sensei: "A true speller strikes only the right letter."
🎮 **LETTER SLICE** *(new engine: fruit-ninja)* — letters fly up in arcs; **slice them in the target word's order**; slicing wrong letters breaks your combo; bomb-moths must NOT be sliced. Combo multipliers, 10 words. Swipe on touch, mouse-drag on desktop. Panda Sensei & Shadow Ninja join.

**10 · Lab: The Falling Formula** — the Great Fizz needs reagents — words, assembled from falling matter.
🎮 **WORD TETRIS** *(new engine)* — letter tiles fall in columns (Tetris-style speed ramp); land them to build words horizontally (3+ letters clear rows and pour into Beaker's flask); the chapter's target: clear 15 words including 3 "formula words" (given as definitions — vocab hook). Brainiac's preview power shows next letters. Beaker & Brainiac join.

**11 · Critter Pond: The Hungry Garden** — the pond's words are scattered as seeds; only the garden snake can gather them.
🎮 **WORD SNAKE** *(new engine: snake)* — classic snake; the board is scattered with letters; **eat the target word's letters in order** (wrong letter = shrink; walls/tail = classic rules). Each completed word grows the garden border with flowers. 10 words. Zoomies & Capy join.

**12 · Arcade: BOSS — Glitch's Betrayal** ⚡ — the Arcade's own spirit sells its tokens to Vex for "infinite lives."
🎮 **SPACE TYPER** *(new engine: typing shooter)* — Space-Invaders view: glitch-sprites descend carrying words; **type a sprite's word to zap it**; Glitch phases in throwing scramble-bombs (type the unscrambled word to deflect). Lose condition: sprites reach the cabinet floor. Beat Glitch — he escapes to Vex with the Master Token. Pixel Pal & Joy Stick join, and the crew realizes Vex is building an ARMY.

## ACT III · THE SHADOW SPREADS (13–18) — *Vex strikes back; the dark chapters*

**13 · Origami: The Unfolding Bridge** — the paper world's bridges unfold behind the crew as Vex's moths chew creases.
🎮 **WORD LADDER** *(new engine)* — cross the gorge by folding paper planks: change **one letter at a time** to turn word into word (CAT→COT→DOT→DOG). Each valid step folds a plank; invalid steps wobble (Crane's power undoes one). 4 ladders, lengths 4→7. Crane & Golden Crane join.

**14 · Vibe World: The Beat Must Drop** — Beat Bot's rhythm keeps the world's words in sync; the Unspelling makes everything arrhythmic.
🎮 **BEAT SPELLER** *(new engine: rhythm)* — letters approach on a beat highway (4 lanes); **tap them on the beat** to spell the word scrolling above; on-beat = perfect (2x), off-beat = okay, miss = static. 6 songs built from the app's sound kit. Beat Bot, Pengu & Boba join.

**15 · Dino World: The Egg Run** — the Rumble Egg must cross the valley before Gnash's locusts arrive.
🎮 **DINO DASH** *(new engine: runner/platformer)* — Mario-ish auto-runner: jump (tap) and duck (swipe down) over rocks, over locust streams, onto ledges; **collect floating eggs** — each egg landed = a quick spell-word to hatch a baby dino that runs with you (visible growing herd = progress bar). 3 zones, checkpointed. Rexy & Raptor join.

**16 · Enchanted: The Hidden Name** — the forest will only reveal its guardian to one who can guess her name.
🎮 **WORD WISP** *(new engine: Wordle)* — guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries; green/gold/grey feedback; 3 rounds (5, 6, then 7 letters — the guardian's true name). Kid-tuned: after 3 misses Wisp whispers the meaning as a clue (vocab hook). Wisp, Fae & Crystal join.

**17 · THE QUIET NIGHT** ⚡ *the dark chapter* — Vex raids camp. **Bumble and Melody are captured.** Bizzy's own name starts fading off her wings.
🎮 **SILENT MAZE** *(Honeycomb Run — night variant)* — tiny light radius, locust patrol cones, zero points: pure stealth to the exit. Five golden flowers hide in the dark — each restores one letter of **B-I-Z-Z-Y** (spell-cards). Scripted rescue by the fireflies' light at the end — hope beat, and Luma-style meaning training passes to **Fae**.

**18 · BOSS: Void Eats the Sky** — the Cosmos goes fully dark; the crew fights for the stars themselves.
🎮 **STARBREAKER** *(new engine: Breakout)* — paddle + ball vs a wall of darkness-bricks; every brick has a letter; clearing bricks that spell the displayed word = multi-ball + star restored to the sky backdrop. Spell 8 constellations back. Victory is bittersweet: Vex takes the Meadow — the saga's home — and broadcasts it.

## ACT IV · THE LONG ROAD (19–24) — *gathering strength for war*

**19 · Wildhearts: The Migration of Words** — Monarch leads the great migration; lost words hide along the flyway.
🎮 **WORD SAFARI** *(new engine: word-search grid)* — a living landscape is a letter grid (8×8); **find and trace 10 hidden words** (themed: animals, weather) before the season timer; found words animate into the creature/thing they name and join the migration. Echo's power: hear any target used in a sentence. Monarch, Echo & Fawn join.

**20 · Elements: The Storm Sorter** — Four-Season Tuesday returns; words are blowing into the wrong seasons.
🎮 **STORM SORT** *(new engine: sort & match)* — words rain down; **flick each to its right bucket** (by meaning category / by part of speech / by spelling pattern — 3 rounds, one per mechanic). Wrong bucket = lightning crack. The vocab-brain chapter. Breeze, Ember, Droplet & Zappy join.

**21 · Legends: The Riddle Deep** — the old guardians test the crew's knowledge before lending their strength.
🎮 **RIDDLE GAUNTLET** *(trivia engine, saga wrapper)* — Nessie (water riddles), Griff (sky), Pegasus (speed round): 3 guardians × 7 questions from the trivia bank, each guardian with a signature twist (Nessie: picture clues; Griff: true/false storm; Pegasus: 5-second lightning round). Guardians join.

**22 · Turbo: THE JUNKYARD GRAND PRIX** 🏎️ — the Turbo world's champions won't march — unless someone beats them ON WHEELS.
🎮 **TURBO KART RACE** *(racer engine, kart mode — CARS)* — full 3-lap kart race vs **Rally, Turbo, Nitro and Crash**: drift zones, oil slicks, ramp jumps, item pickups (horn = word card → correct answer fires turbo; shield; magnet). Podium finish recruits the Turbo crew AND their war-rigs for the finale.

**23 · The Word Vault** — ammunition: the Vault of First Words opens only to the hardest spellings.
🎮 **VAULT LOCKS** *(championship spelling)* — 5 locks, each a themed set pulled from **Ahana's live weak-pattern radar** (silent letters, doubles, foreign roots, homophone traps, schwa endings). Each lock earns a First Word — a named finale power-up.

**24 · BOSS: Gnash and the Sentence Fields** — rescue Bumble & Melody from the locust camp where sentences are stripped for parts.
🎮 **SENTENCE FORGE** *(idiom/phrase engine, saga wrapper)* — sneak through 3 camps: at each, rebuild shredded idioms and phrases (drag word-pieces into order; then spell the missing key word) while patrol timers sweep. Last cage: free your friends by spelling their NAMES. Ends with Vex's offer to Bizzy — refused. *"Then watch the last word die."*

## ACT V · EVERY WORD EVER (25–30) — *the face-off*

**25 · The Muster of All Worlds** — every champion answers; the Queen commits the Guard.
🎮 **RELAY OF CHAMPIONS** *(medley)* — 6 rapid 60–90s rounds, each a light version of a recruited world's game (Simon → Slice → Tetris → Snake → Beat → Kart), each introduced by its champion. All fellowship powers permanently active from here.

**26 · The Grey Gauntlet** — the long flight into unspelled territory.
🎮 **KEEP FLYING: GAUNTLET** — hard flight; honey pots are guarded: at each, CHOOSE your challenge — spell / trivia / idiom / type / unscramble (play to your strength; gems for choosing your weakness — growth nudge).

**27 · Into the Engine** — descent through the machine that eats words.
🎮 **THE DESCENT** *(maze × tetris hybrid)* — alternating floors: stealth-maze floors (patrol cones) and crusher floors (word-tetris under a descending ceiling — clear rows to keep headroom). 6 floors down to the core.

**28 · The Static** — the Engine's voice must be silenced before Vex can be reached.
🎮 **TYPE-STORM** *(typing shooter, hard)* — The Static screams scrambled words in waves; unscramble-then-type under fire; final phase inverts the screen colors and reverses word order — the hardest pure-skill fight in the saga.

**29 · THE FACE-OFF, PART I** ⚡ *the Infinity-War beat* — full assault. The crew breaches the walls; Bizzy reaches the core.
🎮 Three-phase: kart charge (60s) → flight through collapsing halls (90s) → spell-duel vs Vex... **which cannot be won.** Scripted: the Engine fires, the world greys, friends' portraits fade one by one, Bizzy's last letter goes out. Black screen. *"to be continued…"* (The game tells the player afterward this loss is the story — stars still awarded.)

**30 · THE FACE-OFF, PART II: The Respelling** ⚡ *the Endgame beat* — grey silence. One thing survived: every word Ahana ever spelled in this app, stored in the Stinger Quill. Bizzy begins to speak them.
🎮 **THE RESPELLING** — the ultimate medley: each correct challenge **respells a friend back into existence** (their portrait re-colors, their theme sting plays) — maze beat, flight beat, race beat, Simon beat, slice beat, quiz beat — until the full fellowship stands. Then the final duel: pure spelling, 10 words rising to championship tier, each word visibly re-coloring a slice of the world panorama… ending on the last word: **TOGETHER**. Vex's own words are returned to him — he remembers loving them. Credits roll over all 150 avatars waving. Saga badge: **"Every Word Ever."**

## SYSTEMS
- **Words**: all spell-cards draw from the live corpus-backed difficulty pools; Vault (23) + finale (30) pull from the personal miss-log/weak-pattern radar. Trivia/idioms/vocab/typing use their existing banks through saga wrappers.
- **Fellowship powers**: each recruit's power is a real gameplay modifier (listed above), auto-active in later chapters — recruiting FEELS like getting stronger.
- **Story Gems**: 1 per chapter (30) on a saga shelf + act medallions; finale badge. Coins/karma per existing economy.
- **Assist ladder**: every arcade game: 3 fails → Story Assist (shorter/slower/wider) → 6 fails → Skip-with-story (chapter marked ☆, replayable). A 9-year-old is never walled; a champ can chase 3-star runs.
- **Difficulty**: easy/medium/hard/champ picker applies to all engines (existing system).
- **Side Tales**: the 14 existing per-pack mini-seasons remain, relabeled, off the main path.
