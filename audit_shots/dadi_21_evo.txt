How Karma works
One word spelled right = 1 Karma — in Word Coach, the Arcade, Concepts, anywhere. Karma is your practice record: it only grows and is never spent.
Coins 🪙 are different — they're treasure you win and spend in the Store on worlds, avatars and power-ups. Spending coins never touches your Karma or your evolution.
