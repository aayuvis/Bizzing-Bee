IDIOM
🌍 India
“to beat your own drum”
to announce or brag loudly
Literally: ढिंढोरा पीटना
📜 True story Hindi 'ढिंढोरा पीटना' — town criers beat drums before proclamations.
“He beat his own drum about one lucky win.”
Same idea elsewhere: English: “blow your own trumpet”
