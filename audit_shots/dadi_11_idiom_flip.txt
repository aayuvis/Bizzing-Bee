“drop by drop fills the pot”
small steady efforts accumulate
Literally: बूँद बूँद से घड़ा भरता है
📜 True story Sanskrit/Hindi 'बूँद-बूँद से घड़ा भरता है' — the water-jar version of compound interest.
“Ten words a day — drop by drop fills the pot.”
Same idea elsewhere: Turkish: “drop by drop a lake forms”
